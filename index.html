ViroPredict 1.0

The ViroPredict 1.0 was used to construct a nomogram to predict the possibility of benefit from VG161 of an HCC patient who received OV by incorporating the input of five independent risk genes(i.e. SEZ6, AKR1C1, LILRA5, NCKAP5, and SETD9). The platform utilized the expression of input values to calculate the score of each risk factor gene, as well as a total score (i.e. sum of the gene score), which was negatively correlated with the benefit that will be received from the OV treatment.
